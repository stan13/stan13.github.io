COMMON QUESTIONS

Q. What happens if I don't get along with my tutor?

Everyone learns in different ways and sometimes a tutor might not be the right fit. Our tutors understand this and we are more than happy to trial different tutors until we find the right fit for you.

Q. Is tutoring worthwhile?

Tutoring entails more than just helping students with their homework. For that reason, tutoring is definitely worthwhile! Tuition services can help a student grow in confidence in their own ability, whilst having the available support and advice to help clear up any difficulties they may be experiencing.

Q. Why West Coast Tuition?

West Coast Tuition tutors are hand-picked based on their own achievements at school and are all very experienced with helping high school students in their academic challenges. The large majority of our tutors scored subject prizes and attained an ATAR of above 99. We are reliable and approachable tutors that are prepared for every upcoming session. We draw on our own challenges from high school and learning methods that we incorporated to help each student maximise their academic potential.
Additionally, if your regular tutor is unavailable, we can easily find a replacement to ensure you don't miss out on the crucial help you need the night before a test!

Q. Can we have one trial session before we commit?

Of course! We encourage every student to have a 'trial' session to see if they benefit from our services. We do not expect any further commitments and understand if students do not feel comfortable for any particular reason. Furthermore, students may only want help before an assessment and that is ok with us too.

Q. Can we cancel a session if we're busy?

Yes, we ask that you give the tutor at least 24 hours notice so they can rearrange their schedule.

Q. How do we contact the tutor?

Once you're happy with the tutor you've been allocated, we encourage all weekly communication to be with the tutor directly. They will contact you prior to your first session and give you their contact details. You are also welcome to contact any of the management team for any reason.

Q. How do we pay for sessions?

Once you begin having sessions a monthly invoice will be sent to your email address with directions on how to pay via bank transfer.

Q. How long does a session go for?

That's up to you. Normally sessions last for 1 hour but some students may want longer for multiple subjects or to prepare for an assessment.

Q. What subjects do you offer?

We currently have tutors for the following subjects:

  • Maths (All levels)
  • English
  • English Literature
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Science
  • Music
If you need help with a subject thats not on this list send us a request and we'll find a tutor just for you!
